This is my blog, this is my story.


I get carried away with this brush cleaning. Let's do it again then, what the heck. If I paint something, I don't want to have to explain what it is. You can create beautiful things - but you have to see them in your mind first. When you do it your way you can go anywhere you choose. A happy cloud.

And I will hypnotize that just a little bit. If these lines aren't straight, your water's going to run right out of your painting and get your floor wet. Mix your color marbly don't mix it dead. As trees get older they lose their chlorophyll.

Paint anything you want on the canvas. Create your own world. It looks so good, I might as well not stop. Everything is happy if you choose to make it that way. All you need is a dream in your heart, and an almighty knife.
